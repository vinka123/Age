Simple Age and Grade determiner program

Hello!The first program works by taking your age and evaluates it through an instruction I have given the computer(function).The app works by a condition e.g if age entered is equal or greater than 20,then a pop up message is seen saying "you are going to the club".

The second programm takes your marks and assigns it a grade(E to A)

    (0-20)Grade E
    (21-39)Grade D
    (40-59)Grade C
    (60-79)Grade B
    (80-100)Grade A

If you typed for example 64,the browser executes "Grade B".
